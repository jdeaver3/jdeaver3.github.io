Website Evaluation

Yale School of Art

The Yale School of Art website is very cluttered and unorganized. It does not follow any of the CRAP principles.

  • Contrast: Yale School of Art's contrast is very poor. It has a lot of bright colors and the font size is not consistant
  • Repetition: The wesbite is not cosistant. There is very little repition
  • Alignment: A lot of the alignment in this website is off.
  • Proximity: It is hard to differentiate what elements are related due to the poor placement of elements

Zillow

Zillow's website is easy to read and navigate. It is a well designed website that follows all of the CRAP principles very well.

  • Contrast: The contrast on this website is very good. Most text is either black or blue and it is easy to read on the white background
  • Repetition: Zillow is very consistant with its colors and designs
  • Alignment: Everything on Zillow is aligned very good
  • Proximity: Elements that are related are place near each other and the website is very well organized
